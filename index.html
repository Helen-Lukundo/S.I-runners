How to play

Move lanes: ← →  or  A D  (swipe left/right)
Jump: ↑ / W / Space (swipe up) — clears low barriers
Slide: ↓ / S (swipe down) — ducks under overhead bars

Dodge walls, cars, barriers and bars.
Grab coins (+1), chests (+25), and life stones (pink gems) that bank an extra life — crash with a life and you revive.
