# S.I Runners 3D A 3D neon-cyberpunk endless runner. Switch lanes, jump barriers, grab coins, and spend them in the shop on new cyberpunk robots. Controls - Move: arrow keys / A D (swipe left-right on phone) - Jump: up arrow / W / Space (swipe up on phone) Shop - Coins you collect are saved on your device. - Spend them to unlock VEX, NOVA, ZENITH, GLITCH, PHANTOM. - Tap SELECT to switch your robot. Open index.html in a browser to play. Created by Sean MC.
